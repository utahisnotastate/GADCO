Introduction

{{show.rand}}The Grand Approach philosophy is full customization leads to one-of-a-kind doors that will give your home, business, or structure the level of elegance and style it deserves.
Below you will see an introduction to all of our currently available customization options. If you are interested in more options, drop us a line. Our network of builders and hardware distributors provide us with a very high level of flexibility for countless configurations and options.

Wood Species

Using only high quality hardwood species and the highest quality cuts of those species provide a truly luxurious door. We currently offer products made in Mahogany, Alder, and Pacific Cherry and have plans to add additional species as they become available.

Stain Types

We have a dozen stock stain colors ranging from Blonde to Ebony with many shades in between. We are sure we can match any interior or exterior color schemes and will provide you with stain samples with which to select the perfect stain. Additionally, we have a number of glazes to increase the shine of your door.


If you have a custom stain color you would like to use, send us a sample and we will create a matching sample for you to confirm. Alternatively, we are able to provide unfinished products if you want to stain the door yourself. We can also paint your doors a number of different paint colors to assist you in creating the perfect door.

Sidelights and Transoms

Sidelights are the vertical windows that sit next to a door, and transoms are the windows that sit on top. We have many different configurations of both sidelights and transoms that can increase the natural light of your foyer and add immensely to the elegance of your home. Configurations include single or dual sidelights and full wrap around sidelights and transoms.

Wrought Iron and Iron Accessories

Wrought Iron
Our wrought iron comes in a variety of thicknesses and textures in both square and circular shapes. They can also be created in black, copper, or silver colors with smooth, antique, or rustic finishes.
Iron Accessories
The best way to accent your rich mahogany door or the wrought iron on it is through our iron accessories. We have tons of options including of clavos, iron strapping, speakeasy door covers, or wrought iron accents.

Glass Types

In addition to plain clear glass and clear DP50 glass, we also offer two-way glass, eight different colors, twelve different textures, and three colored texture glass types. We also have artisan rice paper that can be sandwiched in between the two panes of glass providing a unique look with additional privacy.

Glass Etching

We also provide glass etching. We can etch anything you would like into the glass of your door.

Wood Molding

Our trim, interior, and exterior molding come in the standard forms below, but we can custom cut any style of molding you desire and can work with you in selecting specific molding from our catalog. We can even hand carve a few styles of molding to fit your wine cellar door or beach house.

Degrees of Distressing

Wood distressing is offered in five different degrees from minor distressing to severe distressing, giving your door as much of a rustic or antique look as you want.

Hand Carving

Last but not least, we have a handful of master craftsman that are capable of adding hand carving of anything you want. Family shields, beach landscapes, or simply ornate patterns are common selections among our clients.

Hardware

Generally, our door slabs are not prepped for any hardware and our pre-hung doors are only prepped for the included hinges; however, we can be involved as much or as little as you want when it comes to hardware. Our network of hardware suppliers are capable of supplying a wide range of unique and elegant hardware. The doors can be prepped or the hardware installed at our workshop, it all depends on your requirements.

Conclusion
